Best Chicken Salad

Description

Chicken salad served with toasted almonds, and celery. Perfect for any hot or warm summer day. Great for when going on picnic on any sunny day. Also lovely when added on a sandwich or a a dip for chips. With each bite being as flavorful as the last.

Ingredients

  • ½ cup blanched slivered almonds
  • ½ cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 2 cups chopped, cooked chicken meat
  • 1 stalk celery, chopped

Steps

  1. Place almonds in a frying pan. Toast over medium-high heat, shaking frequently. Watch carefully, as they burn easily.
  2. Mix together mayonnaise, lemon juice, and pepper in a medium bowl. Toss with chicken, toasted almonds, and celery.
